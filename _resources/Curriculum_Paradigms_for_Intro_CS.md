## Curricular Paradigms For Intro CS

We discussed a variety of different approaches to intro CS.  Perhaps people can add to this list and add links to courses and advice about how to succeed at each of these or how to decide among them.

- Problem solving using CS; domains are "on the side"
   - clobbers non-majors, prepares for upper years
- theory-first
- hardware-first
- functions-first
- ...
- media computation (computation on images and sound)
- Alice and programming through the creative process
- vehicles for intro CS
   - robots
   - communication
   - networking
   - science
- discrete mathematics and programming mixed
