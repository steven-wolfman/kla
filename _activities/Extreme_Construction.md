## Extreme Construction

This KLA is transcribed from the SIGCSE 2004 Special Session on KLAs.

## Overview To KLA

## Designed by Joe Bergin

Summary: Learn the XP planning game

Learning Goals: to be added

Course And Level: This is appropriate for a sophomore or higher class level.

Class Size: The class should be between 10-30 people.

Preparation Time: One hour of preparation time is needed.

Execution Time: The exercise will take 4 hours and one hour of debriefing is also needed.


## Planning For KLA

Materials: Play dough, paper, glue, scissors (kindergarten art supplies), 3X5 cards, and pens.

Preparation: The instructor must first talk about XP.

## Execution Of KLA

Description: Designate a "customer" who writes "features" on cards for a "product."  "Developers" estimate how long to build in minutes.  "Customer" then prioritizes cards (some, not all).  Developers build just those features.  Iterate many times.  Example: If the customer wants a "castle." The features are a "moat, 12 high walls, and dragon."

Variants And Extra Topics: below

- Waterfall  
- Use lego mindstorms  
- Mine patterns target the practice
